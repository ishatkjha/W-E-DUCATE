Mentor Me India

Mentor Me India program is a one-to-one community-based mentorship model similar to the Big Brothers Big Sisters in the US, but carefully tailored to the Indian context. We are unique in India in that we focus exclusively on mentoring younger children through a one-to-one relationship within a community setting. This program provides extensive mentoring and support structure through intensive training and parental engagement, a month-long orientation, and quarterly group mentoring sessions. The Mentorship Project focuses on one to one mentorship for children from low income families in India.

The India Mentors

TheIndianMentors.com Mentorship Program is well defined and engaging process between the mentor and the mentee, who will interact with each other in a structured program as follows: Mentor-Mentee matching, Building Connection, Motivation and Develop/Progress. All the training given to the mentees by the mentors will be tracked/assessed by the Project Co-ordinators and the mentor itself to see the progress in his/her mentees. A feedback mechanism will be there for the mentors/mentees from the project team upon their performance and their learnings as well.
